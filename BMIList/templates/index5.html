About

Hi! I'm Milleth, i'm a thrid year college student currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Industrial Education at Technological University of the Philippines-Cavite Campus. Right now, i'm a fitness enthusiast so I decided to make myself fit by doing workouts at home.

Since the start of the coronavirus pandemic, it's become even harder to stick to a solid routine. With most gyms closed, the fitness world for most of us has shifted to at-home or outdoor workouts. So I made this website to force us to self-motivate even more as we've found alternative workout routines.

I believe fitness should be accessible to everyone, everywhere, regardless of income level or access to a gym. That's why I offer free, full-length workout videos, the most affordable and eefective workout programs on the web.


My main goal is to make health and fitness ATTAINABLE, AFFORDABLE AND APPROACHABLE.
